How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles of the same image touch, they merge into one!
Note: The images on the tiles are intended to represent the interests, quirkiness, and awesomeness of the MIT Class of 2018.

Created by Margaret Yu. Inspired by an obsession with the original 2048 game, MIT Class of 2018, and GetMIT! by Mitchell Gu. Based on 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli and 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
